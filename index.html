About

Hi! I'm Mingyu Wang.
I'm a Researcher, Civil Engineer, and AI Enthusiast who aspires to become an Educator and a Builder of Smarter Infrastructures.

I am currently a doctoral student at the School of Civil Engineering, Southeast University. My research focuses on Structural Health Monitoring and Neural Operators. I am passionate about empowering traditional engineering with AI intelligence to create the next generation of resilient infrastructure.

Feel free to drop me an email for collaboration.

Research Interests

  • Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)
    • Focusing on high-speed railway bridges and infrastructure safety.
  • Neural Operators
    • Leveraging deep learning models (FNO, DeepONet) to accelerate solving partial differential equations in engineering.
  • Scientific Machine Learning
    • Data-driven modeling for complex structural dynamics.
  • Finite Element Analysis
    • Numerical simulation for structural mechanics and multi-physics problems.
